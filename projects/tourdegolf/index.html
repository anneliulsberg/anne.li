Tour de Golf

Brochure for Tour de Golf finale in Portugal.

About the project

The client needed to get a small A6 brochure designed for the upcoming Tour de Golf finale in Portugal.

Challenges

This small A6 borchure needed to contain a lot of information and the challenge was to get it all in without making it messy.

Solution

To maintain order in this brochure I used colors and pictures to add some air in the content. The text is neatly organised in colums and sections and the logos are sorted after size to balance out all the visual noise.
